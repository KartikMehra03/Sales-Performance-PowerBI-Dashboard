# Sales-Performance-PowerBI-Dashboard
**Sales-Performance-Analysis**: It is an interactive Power BI dashboard that presents an end-to-end Sales Performance Analysis using Power BI. The goal is to analyze sales trends, customer behavior and regional performance to generate actionable business insights.

🛠️ **Tech Stack**

• 📊 **Power BI Desktop**– Data Visualization platform used for report creation.

• 📂 **Power Query**– Data transformation platform for cleaning, reshaping and preparing the data.

• 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures.

📌 **Features / Highlights**

🎯 **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
• **Total Sales:** $2.26M
• **Total Orders:** 4,922
• **Average Order Value:** $459

👁️ **Key Visual**

• **Sales Over Time (Line Chart)**: Displays yearly sales trend highlighting growth from $459K (2016) to $722K (2018).

• **Category-Wise Sales (Bar Chart)**: Compares performance across categories with Technology leading at $0.83M, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies.

• **Sales by Ship Mode (Column Chart)**: Shows distribution of sales by shipping type where Standard Class dominates with $1.34M.

• **Sales by Segment (Donut Chart)**: Visualizes customer contribution with Consumer segment contributing 50.76% ($1.15M) of total sales.

• **Sales by Region (Map Visualization)**: Highlights geographical performance identifying the West region as the top-performing market.

💡**Insights and Conclusions**

• Total sales of $2.26M from 4,922 orders with an average order value of $459.
• Sales increased from $459K (2016) to $722K (2018) showing strong growth.
• Technology is the top category ($0.83M), followed by Furniture and Office Supplies.
• Consumer segment contributes 50.76% ($1.15M) of total sales.
• Standard Class shipping dominates ($1.34M), indicating preference for cost-effective delivery.
• West region is the highest-performing market.
